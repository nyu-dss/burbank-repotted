Relationship between Gender and Literacy, Defendants

 

When it comes to defendants, the picture is similar – male defendants are more likely to be literate than female defendants. However, the correlation is somewhat weaker than that for the plaintiffs. The difference in literacy between male and female defendants is significant, but is not as strong as the difference between male and female plaintiffs.
