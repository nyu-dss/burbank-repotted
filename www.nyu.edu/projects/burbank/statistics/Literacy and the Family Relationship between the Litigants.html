Literacy and the Family Relationship between the Litigants

 

Family members suing each other were much more likely to both be illiterate than non-related participants. In almost half of the family cases when the literacy of both the plaintiff and the defendant was recorded, neither side was literate. The same was true for only 16% of the non-family cases.
